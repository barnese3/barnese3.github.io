The Inn at Crystal Cove

Located in the quaint seaside community of Winthrop, Massachusetts, just minutes from Logan International Airport and a short distance by car or public transportation from Downtown Boston, The Inn at Crystal Cove boasts comfortable accommodations and a stunning unobstructed view of the Boston Harbor and city skyline.

Since the Inn will be your "home away from home" during your travels, we provide complete living facilities, including a fully-equipped kitchen for each of our two buildings, guest lounges, pool, free parking, cable television, free local telephone calling and free wireless internet.

We invite you to come enjoy the seaside atmosphere here in our idyllic corner of New England! Our nightly rates range from $109 to $299. For more information or to make your reservations, please contact us at 1(617) 846-9217. Out-of-state guests can reach us toll free at 1(877) 966-8447.

The Inn is uniquely situated just feet from Boston Harbor and just 175 yards east of the open ocean, making it the ideal place for swimming or for long ocean strolls on the adjacent sidewalks. Several miles to the north is Revere Beach--America's first public beach, with miles of walkable promenades and an eclectic population.

A short distance from the Inn is Point Shirley, site of the state-of-the-art Massachusetts Water Resource Authority, featuring a spectacular 3-mile walking, jogging, and biking trail along the ocean that offers incredible views of the harbor, islands, and downtown Boston. More information about the area can be found at the Winthrop Chamber of Commerce's website.
